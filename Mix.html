A Comparison Of The Goals

Health & Well-Being and Clean Water & Sanitation are very closely tied together. One example of this is that to positively impact ones health, they should try and drink water to stay hydrated, while on the flip-side, if you are drinking water, however it isn't clean, it can lead to illnesses which will negatively impact ones health. As well, to have a better well-being and to improve your mental health, making sure that you are drinking enough and that you are hydrated really does make a difference. Or if one feels nervous, splashing some water on your face can help.

How To Help

Health & Well-Being

If you would like to help with Health & Well Being around the world, there are several charitable organizations that you could donate to, or donating directly to a hospital.

For a regional option, donating to Sick Kids is an option. On their website, you have the options to directly donate, or start a fundraiser.

For a national option, donating to The Canadian Red Cross is a charity which is a part of the Red Cross foundation and helps in many ways.

For an international option, donating to Doctors Without Borders is an organizations that make positive impacts all over the world.

Clean Water & Sanitation

If you would like to help with Clean Water & Sanitation around the world, there are several charitable organizations that you could donate to.

For a national option, donating to Water First is an organization what works directly with Canadian First Nation communities to give access to clean and safe water.

For an international option, donating to Charity: Water or donating to Water.org are both charities which work on providing safe and accessible water worldwide.
